  • Carly Grizzaffi

Carly Grizzaffi is a not only a dedicated student but devotes herself to anything she sets her mind to. In 2019, a magnificent opportunity arose to join the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program hosted at Goldman Sachs. She spent the seven weeks learning countless skills and grew not only as a programmer but as a person. Aside from her love of tech, Carly is a Varsity swimmer, Model UN and debate team participant, editor, producer and director for her schools TV channel, peer leader, tour guide for her high school, in addition to countless other activities. Moreover, she keeps herself occupied running a non-profit organization she Co-founded called Mission Possible that aids families in need by fixing their homes.
